Introduction

This website showcases my professional readiness for IT Supervisor or entry-level management roles in the U.S. technology industry. Built as part of my Information Technology Field Experience (EX20) at Cumberland University, it reflects the competencies and leadership skills developed throughout my academic journey under the guidance of Professor Michael Moifolley.

My portfolio includes leadership templates, project documentation, strategic plans, and operational tools used to manage teams and performance in an enterprise environment. The site demonstrates not only my technical understanding, but also my communication, planning, and ethical leadership approach.

Supervisor Vision

As an IT Supervisor, I will lead with integrity and foster innovation. I value high-performing, diverse teams built on collaboration, accountability, and strategic alignment. My leadership approach is grounded in ethical practices, service excellence, and delivering results that support organizational success and continuous improvement.
